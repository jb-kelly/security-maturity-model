This assessment gives you a precise read on where you are across artifact management, supply chain security, and regulatory alignment, and maps a clear path to where you need to be.

The six levels
0 Ad-hoc — No centralized registry or controls
1 Foundational — Basic registry, minimal access control
2 Repeatable — Scanning, proxying, and RBAC in place
3 Defined — Signing, SBOMs, and policy-gated promotion
4 Managed — Automated remediation and full audit trails
5 Optimized — Cryptographic provenance, hermetic builds
